Student project - 12/2019

Game & Level Design


Having completed a master's degree in Fine Arts with a specialization in Game Design and Licence Professionnelle en Game et Level Design, I have acquired all the tools needed to create a coherent and meaningful design for different genres of games. I am also acquainted with the "coziness" concept and the procedural rhetoric which I have studied in depth throughout my academic years.

  • Article 1: Game Jam Survival Kit
  • Article 2: Fine Arts and VR (in French)

Development

I have extensive experience working with Unity, having developed most of my games on this platform. In addition to being proficient with its standard features, I am also skilled in utilizing its advanced tools such as Pro Builder, Tilemap system, Post Processing, and the particle system. While programming is not my primary expertise, my proficiency in C# programming enables me to collaborate seamlessly with the programming team.

Furthermore, I have actively engaged with Unreal Engine, gaining practical experience through hands-on projects and continuous learning to proficiently navigate its tools and functionalities. These technical skills, combined with my ability to work both independently and as part of a team, allow me to communicate effectively and deliver high-quality work.

Softwares, Tools and More

  • Art: Adobe suite, Figma and Blender
  • Game engines: Unity and Unreal Engine
  • Programming/markup languages: C# (intermediate), HTML and CSS/JavaScript (basic)
  • Project management: Trello, Jira and Notion
  • Source control: GitHub and SourceTree
  • VFX: Unity's Particle System
  • Video editing: Adobe Première

Besides these skills, I am used to the agile development methodology. As a game designer, I aim to create meaningful and fun games as a result of a properly designed procedural rhetoric. I am also an active member of the collective Game Impact where we discuss a better and more progressive way to make meaningful games. If you want to learn more about my game design experience, please check my Upwork profile.
