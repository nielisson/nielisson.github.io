Game Design


Having completed a master's degree in Fine Arts with a specialization in Game Design, I have acquired all the tools needed to create a coherent and meaningful design for different genres of games. I am very fond of the "coziness" concept and the procedural rhetoric which I have studied in depth throughout my academic years.

  • Featured Game Design Documents
  • Article: Game Jam Survival Kit
  • Game Jams I hosted

Unity

Most of my games were made on Unity, so I am quite capable on this software. Besides the standard functionalities, I am also familiar with Pro Builder, Tilemap system, Post Processing and the particle system. Futhermore, I have learned how to code in C#, which allows me to assist the programmer. That also provided me the vocabulary and the clarity needed to communicate my ideas to the team.
